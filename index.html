About Me

Drake Smith is a musician, music industry professional, and web developer-in-training from Los Angeles, CA. Drake graduated from the University of Southern California in 2013 with a Bachelor of Music, Jazz Studies - Trombone. Drake performs as a trombonist regularly throughout Southern California and performs a wide variety of genres, from jazz, pop and funk.

Though Drake is a die-hard USC Trojan, he is temporariliy halting the intense rivalry with UCLA in order to attend the UCLA Extension Coding Boot Camp. His goal is to start a career in web development soon after completion of the program. In his free time, Drake enjoys attending music festivals and concerts, following college football, NFL, and the NBA, eating Asian food, and hiking.
